What is PTSD?

Post-Traumatic Stress Disorder (PTSD) is a condition triggered by experiencing or witnessing a terrifying event. It can lead to long-lasting mental and emotional effects.

Common Symptoms:

  • Flashbacks or nightmares about the event
  • Avoidance of reminders or triggers
  • Hypervigilance and being easily startled
  • Mood changes or feeling detached from others

What Can Help:

  • Trauma-focused therapy (e.g., EMDR, CBT)
  • Support groups and peer support
  • Medication to manage symptoms
  • Mindfulness and grounding techniques
