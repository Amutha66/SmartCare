What is Bipolar Disorder?

Bipolar disorder is a mental health condition marked by extreme mood swings, including emotional highs (mania or hypomania) and lows (depression).

Common Symptoms:

  • Periods of unusually intense emotion
  • Sleep disturbances or racing thoughts
  • Risk-taking or impulsive behavior during manic episodes
  • Prolonged sadness or lack of energy during depressive episodes

What Can Help:

  • Medication (e.g., mood stabilizers, antipsychotics)
  • Psychotherapy (CBT, interpersonal therapy)
  • Structured routines and sleep schedules
  • Support networks and psychoeducation
